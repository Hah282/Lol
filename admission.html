About Senior High School


Sta. Lucia Senior High School offers specialized programs in Grade 11-12 that are tailored to equip students with the skills needed for further education and employment.

Admission Criteria for Grade 11-12


  • Completed online application form
  • Certified copy of Junior High School completion certificate
  • Grade 10 report card (Form 138)
  • Certificate of Good Moral Character
  • Entrance exam or interview

Available Tracks


  • ICT - Programming: Focused on programming languages and software development
  • ICT - Animation: Emphasis on digital animation and multimedia
  • CSS: Consumer Services Skills
  • ABM: Accountancy, Business, and Management
  • Cookery: Culinary arts and food preparation skills

Application Process


  1. Fill out the online application form.
  2. Submit all required documents.
  3. Schedule an entrance exam or interview.
  4. Receive your admission decision within two weeks.
